## 4. Spatial-apparatus friction notes (first exercise)

The apparatus WORKS — every EC3 above that cites Bachelder was read as
`local → from_local() → pixel crop → labeled bar vs terrain feature`,
with radii per the spatial-evidence guidance. Friction found:

1. **No crop tool in the repo.** This pass improvised a scratchpad
   script (Pillow decode of the JP2 masters + `SheetGeoref.from_local`
   + crosshair/scale-bar annotation). Recommend committing a
   `reconstruction/scripts/crop_sheet.py` next maintenance pass — the
   dossier author workflow in spatial-evidence.md §"How a dossier
   author consumes this" assumes the capability but nothing ships it.
2. **API naming**: the doc's example calls `to_local(px, py)`;
   the inverse an author actually needs first is `from_local(lx, lz)`
   (present, undocumented in the consumption guidance). Doc patch item.
3. **Pillow needs `MAX_IMAGE_PIXELS` lifted** for the ~100 MP sheets
   (decompression-bomb guard) — worth a note in the guidance.
4. **Label-vs-bar ambiguity**: Bachelder places name labels 50-250 m
   east of the drawn battery bars on the Union side (and often west on
   the CSA side); reading the LABEL coordinate instead of the BAR adds
   a systematic offset larger than the registration error. The
   guidance table should add: "read the bar, cite the label."
5. **Drawn-state semantics matter more than the printed window**: j3-03
   (1-5 PM) draws mid/post-assault states — e.g. Dearing's batteries
   appear on the forward road-crest line (the daybreak-advance +
   post-assault state), Brown's battery appears twice (line + rear
   park), Arnold's bar sits ~150 m ESE of the wall near the Leister
   house (a withdrawn/refit state bearing on ED-7 — recorded on no
   dossier this pass since Arnold's is pass-1 closed; noted here). The
   spatial-evidence "time envelope" bullet covers this in principle;
   in practice every anchor this pass needed an explicit drawn-state
   note.
6. **Register gaps surfaced by use**: no marker entries for Little
   Round Top units (Rittenhouse read from the sheet + tablet DMS
   instead) and no monuments.json coverage south of the Stannard
   front or on the hills; the 51-marker sector boundary is exactly the
   pass-2 unit set's boundary. Fill list handed to the register
   maintenance item.
7. **What worked well**: the similarity transforms round-tripped
   within expectation everywhere checked (Cowan monument vs sheet
   label vs build hold all within ~20 m); DMS→local conversion via
   pyproj matched the register's own conversions; and the low-ground
   battery LIST on the sheet was legible enough to advance an open ED
   — the "cartographic authority" role the program claimed is real.
