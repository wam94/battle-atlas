Chain role: **the CA-J2E-1 order's issuing echelon and the
absent-then-returning frame** — the command record of the corps that
left Culp's Hill, went to the left, touched the ED-61 ground (the
Lockwood recapture), and came back to find its works occupied. Command

  the RIGHT WING July 1-3 (Meade's disposition); **Brig. Gen. Alpheus
  S. Williams commanded the corps** in that interval, Brig. Gen.
  Thomas H. Ruger the 1st Division, Col. Silas Colgrove Ruger's
  brigade (or-27-1-williams-aw p. 773 — the cascade verbatim;
  or-27-1-slocum). A three-deep temporary-command stack: the return
  (p. 184) still heads the corps "Maj. Gen. HENRY W. SLOCUM" with

## EC4 — Movement legs

1. **The detachment** [D trigger = the left-wing crisis; stated 6.30
   at Greene's grain]: Ruger's division + Lockwood to the left,
   Geary "to cover and defend the intrenched line of the whole
   corps" (Williams p. 774) — the order Geary's execution turned
   into the wrong-road march (us-xii-2-hq-geary.md).
2. **The left-wing round trip** [D]: Williams marched "following as
   nearly as possible the direction of the heavy firing"; met
   McGilvery in person; Lockwood deployed and "recaptur[ed] three
   pieces of artillery abandoned by the enemy in his retreat";
   Ruger's two lines through the woods on Lockwood's left; **"It was
   now quite dark. I therefore ordered both commands to halt"**
   [B-adjacent: after ~19:59 civil-twilight end, ED-31] — then the
   return order.
3. The return and the discovery [D→C]: works found occupied
   (Colgrove/Ruger side carried at register note grain); Williams
   learned Geary's true position "nearly 12 o'clock at night" and
   got Slocum's daylight-attack order.

## EC5 — Activity record

1. The breastworks decision [untimed, credited]: Slocum p. 759 —
   works + abatis "enabled us at a critical moment to detach portions
   of the command" (the CA-J2E-1 order's own precondition, stated at
   corps grain).
2. **The ED-61 touch**: Williams's McGilvery meeting + the Lockwood
   three-pieces recapture — a command-grain member of the adopted
   recovery ledger (angle-editorial-decisions.md ED-61).
3. July 3 morning plan [C via or-27-1-williams-aw]: artillery at
   daylight, 15 minutes by arrangement, "the enemy, without waiting
   our assault, themselves attacked" — the seven-hour fight frame
   (CA-J3M context for pass 9+).
