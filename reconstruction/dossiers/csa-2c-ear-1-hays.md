2. **The assault** [A vs CA-J2E-3; the chain's trigger primary on

## EC5 — Activity record

1. **The summit moment** [A]: "by a simultaneous rush from my whole
   line, I captured several pieces of artillery, four stand of
   colors, and a number of prisoners. At that time every piece of
   artillery which had been firing upon us was silenced" — the
   guns-possession claim rides ED-63's proposed convergent treatment
   (Wiedrich's intrenchments fight + Ricketts's spiked left piece are
   the same event from inside; **no gun was carried off — the
   trophies brought off are the four colors**, his own wording).
2. **The counterattack reception** [D]: fire reserved through two-
   three volleys ("I had been cautioned to expect friends both in
   front, to the right, and to the left" — Longstreet/Rodes/Johnson
   named as the expected supports; the caution that cost the first
   volleys), then the three-deep lines (Carroll's front, both-sided
   with us-ii-3-1-carroll.md) forced the retire.
3. July 1 [C]: the town fight (drove "entirely out of the city");
   the two-Napoleon joint capture with Avery's brigade — **Early's
   own convergent-credit ruling carried on both dossiers**.
4. July 3 [E]: town-street sharpshooter duty; no assault role.
