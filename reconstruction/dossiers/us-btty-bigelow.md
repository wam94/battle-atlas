## EC4 — Movement legs

1. The 16:00 move to Sickles's front [C +45 class].
2. **The prolonge retirement** [D trigger = the III Corps line's
   collapse]: "prolonges were fixed, and firing at skirmishers on
   our left and the approaching line of battle on our right, we
   retired 300 yards" — firing-while-retiring, the drill-book
   delaying leg, executor-attested from both echelons (McGilvery:
   "Captain Bigelow retired by prolonge, firing canister").
3. **The stand and the loss** [D]: at the angle, without support,
   enfiladed — "Its flanks closing in on either side of us, obtained
   a cross-fire, which silenced the four pieces on my right, and
   prevented their withdrawal"; Milton's section (two guns) out;
   Bigelow w at the limbers; the battery's survivors out over the
   wall.
4. The recovery [D, ~20:00-21:00]: NOT a battery leg — Dow's
   procured infantry detail hauled the four guns off (McGilvery);
   "Our four guns were recovered the same day" (Milton). ED-61's
   spine.
