## EC5 — Activity record

1. **The Emmitsburg road counter-battery fire** [C ~15:00+,
   report-primary]: "succeeded in silencing several of the enemy's
   guns," though the enemy "soon opened from different points"
   (report, verbatim).
2. **Bucklyn's wound, the caisson-recovery episode** [C, report-
   primary, via Randolph's dossier]: wounded "while recovering a
   caisson" (`us-iii-arty-randolph.md` EC1); command passed to
   Freeborn.
3. **The withdrawal, ~20 minutes after arrival** [D, report-primary]:
   "without infantry support," with the enemy "advancing in line in
   the ravine in front," the battery withdrew under "heavy musketry
   fire" as the enemy came "within a few yards"; one caisson abandoned,
   later recovered. The battery "did not participate in subsequent
   fighting" (report, verbatim/paraphrase) — an attested-static
   remainder for July 3.
