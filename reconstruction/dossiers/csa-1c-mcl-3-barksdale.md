  18th, 21st Mississippi. **Brig. Gen. William Barksdale mw July 2 at
  Plum Run** → **Col. Benjamin G. Humphreys (21st MS)** (or-27-oob;
  tablet).
- **THE BARKSDALE PIN** [D, multiple witnesses]: fell in the Plum Run
  fight where the three left regiments "encountered fresh troops and
  a fierce conflict ensued in which Brig. Gen. Wm. Barksdale fell

  repulse record (us-ii-3-3-willard.md, pass 2); left on the field,

- Pre-step-off character record: "General, let me go; General, let me

  arithmetic — Kershaw+Semmes, "after a further interval of at least
