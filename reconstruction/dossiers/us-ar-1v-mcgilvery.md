## EC5 — Activity record

- **July 3, cannonade — the ordered silence** [A window vs CA-J3A-1/5]:
  under Hunt's conservation order the line withheld/ceased counter-battery
  fire. McGilvery's own report: "About one-half hour after the
  commencement, some general commanding the infantry line ordered three
  of the batteries to return the fire" — which he shut down as against
  orders (or-27-1-mcgilvery p. 884; the interfering general is unnamed in
  the verified text — conflict slot, likely Hancock per the Hunt-Hancock
  dispute record; see us-arty-hq-hunt). His duration statement: the enemy
  "fired about one hour and a half, and expended at least 10,000 rounds
  of ammunition" [C via his report-nominal profile] — primary
  corroboration of CA-J3A-4's envelope.
- **The silence is negative evidence of the first class**: Phillips's
  monument: "About 1:30 by order of Brig. Gen. H.J. Hunt fired on the
  Confederate batteries" then held under the policy (stone-sentinels 5th
  MA page, verified 2026-07-02) — brief authorized counter-battery, then
  quiet until the assault.
- **The repulse fires** [C via or-27-1-mcgilvery p. 884 + A-window vs
  CA-J3A-6..10]: "At about 3 p. m. a line of battle of about 3,000 or
  4,000 men appeared, advancing directly upon our front, which was
  completely broken up and scattered by our fire before coming within
  musket range of our lines. Immediately after, appeared three extended
  lines of battle … by training the whole line of guns obliquely to the
  right, we had a raking fire through all three of these lines" —
  case/shell into Pickett's right, oblique, through the whole advance.
- **Wilcox/Lang smashed** [D after CA-J3A-9]: Phillips's monument:
  "Opened an enfilading fire soon after on Longstreet's advancing line of
  infantry and assisted in repulsing the assault", then the Florida
  brigade's follow-up (stone-sentinels; with the 16th VT's reversal —
  see us-i-3-3-stannard).
- Rounds expended: his "10,000 rounds" is the ENEMY's expenditure as he
  judged it; his own line's expenditure figure not located (flagged).
