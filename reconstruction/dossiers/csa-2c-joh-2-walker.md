## EC5 — Activity record

1. **The evening negative, double-attested**: Walker — "instructions
   from General Johnson to remain on the flank, if I thought it
   necessary. As our flank and rear would have been entirely
   uncovered... I deemed it prudent to hold my position until after
   dark"; Johnson — "General Walker did not arrive in time to
   participate in the assault that night." A DISCRETIONARY negative
   (unlike Garnett's ordered silence): the commander's own reasoning
   is the record.
2. All-day skirmishing July 2 [E]; the 2nd VA action [D] with loss
   "3 wounded" on that front (Winchester-section figure? no — the
   day's total stated in the Gettysburg text; carried as stated).
3. July 3 [C/D]: five hours' incessant firing; "my right, extending
   beyond the breastworks, suffered very heavily"; the refit hour;
   two more advances "with equally bad success"; desultory fire to
   dark.
