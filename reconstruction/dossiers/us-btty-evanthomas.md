## EC1 — Identity & command

- Battery C, 4th U.S. Artillery; **1st Lt. (brevet Maj.) Evan Thomas**
  commanding throughout — son of Lorenzo Thomas (the Army's Adjutant
  General), took command before Antietam and held it through the war,
  breveted captain for Fredericksburg and MAJOR FOR GETTYSBURG
  specifically; killed in action against the Modoc in 1873 (tablet,
  gettysburg.stonesentinels.com, "Battery C, 4th United States
  Artillery," fetch-verified this pass, paraphrase). Guns: **six
  12-pounders** (tablet, verbatim).
