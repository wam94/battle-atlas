## EC1 — Identity & command

- Iverson's Brigade, Rodes's Division, Second Corps: 5th, 12th, 20th,
  23rd North Carolina (or-27-oob). **Brig. Gen. Alfred Iverson**
  through the battle; the remnant "attached... to his brigade
  [Ramseur's]" July 1 evening–July 3; **the quiet relief**: at
  Williamsport "I assumed the duties of provost-marshal... when my
  connection with the brigade ceased" (or-27-2-iverson — command
  ended without a wounding; the record's own understatement).
- Sub-command pins [D]: Capt. D. P. Halsey (AAG) rallied and led the
  remnant's charge (Iverson + Rodes both credit him); Lt. Col. Davis
  held the 12th NC intact; Capt. Benjamin Robinson (5th NC) led the
  sharpshooter corps.

## EC5 — Activity record

1. **The destruction fight** [D]: "a most desperate fight took
   place" at 100 yards; the support miscarriage ledger — Daniel's
   promised regiment formed elsewhere, O'Neal broke, "the enemy,
   being relieved from pressure, charged in overwhelming force"
   (or-27-2-iverson).
2. **THE SURRENDER-AND-EXONERATION RECORD** [D, verbatim]: "When I
   saw white handkerchiefs raised, and my line of battle still lying
   down in position, I characterized the surrender as disgraceful;
   but when I found afterward that 500 of my men were left lying
   dead and wounded... I exonerated, with one or two disgraceful
   individual exceptions, the survivors, and claim for the brigade
   that they nobly fought and died without a man running to the
   rear."
3. The prisoners "numberless... cut off" at the railroad
   [D]; the July 2 night advance "to certain destruction" and his own
   retreat order (with Ramseur's 200-yard battery reconnaissance —
   the same event, csa-2c-rod-4-ramseur.md).
4. Hagerstown July 6 [D]: the ambuscade ("killed, wounded, and
   captured as many of the enemy as I had men", loss 3 k / 6 w).

- **ANV return (p. 342), the wounded cell RE-READ pass 11 (both

  carried as the cell's reading; 382 carried as the

  brigade line** (ED-49 pattern — the 308 m IS the dress-parade
  line's capture mass). Note the regiment k-sum (111) also
  disagrees with the total row's 130 — the row's internal noise is
  systemic, strengthening the pooled-reconstruction reading.
- **THE SHAPE (the exemplar)** [D]: effectively ALL loss in ONE
  episode — the Forney-field line, inside the 14:00–15:00 window;
