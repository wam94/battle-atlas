## EC3 — Position anchors

1. **July 2, arrival ~5 P.M.** [C ~17:00, tablet-primary]: "Arrived
   about 5 P. M. after a march of 33 miles from Manchester Md. Moved
   to the left and at dark to the extreme left with one Regiment the
   5th Vermont on picket" (tablet, verbatim) — the same 33-mile-class
   forced march as the corps's other brigades (Nevin's 34 mi/17 h,
   `us-vi-3-3-nevin.md`; Neill's 33 mi, `us-vi-2-2-neill.md`, this
   pass).
2. **July 3** [C, tablet-primary]: "The Brigade advanced a short
   distance and took position with its right on east slope of Round
   Top its left on the Taneytown Road and remained until the close of
   the battle under no fire except that from artillery" (tablet,
   verbatim) — an explicit attested-static/under-cannonade-only
   statement, the ED-44 present-but-not-firing class.
