## EC5 — Activity record

1. **July 3 cannonade** [C +7]: "At 1 p. m. the enemy opened a fierce
  cannonade upon the line from a hundred or more guns, which was
  continued until nearly 3 p. m." (or-27-1-harrow p. 420) — a second
  clean +7-class divisional clock bracketing CA-J3A-1..5.
2. **The advance** [A window]: "the enemy moved from the woods, 1,000
  yards in front, and steadily advanced to the assault. ... the crest
  of the hill occupied by the right of Colonel Hall and the left of
  General Webb seemed to be the point to which their main attack was
  directed" (p. 420) — the convergence-target statement in the
  division commander's own words.
3. **The melee** [B duration]: "the contest raged with almost
  unparalleled ferocity for nearly an hour" (p. 420) — the division-
  grain duration for advance-through-repulse (13:00+2h+1h nominal ≈
  the 15:05-15:45 canonical fight; consistent at +7).
4. **Aftermath** [D]: "the enemy was routed and fled in disorder";
  "many battle-flags were captured" (p. 420).
